Features

A Complete Virtual Laboratory for African Schools

Designed around the realities of Nigerian secondary education — slow internet, low-end devices, and budget constraints — while delivering world-class STEM learning.

🧪

Live Interactive Simulations

Fully functional Canvas-based simulations for Physics (pendulum, projectile, waves, Ohm's law, optics), Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics with real-time data capture.

📊

Curriculum-Aligned

Every simulation maps to WAEC, NECO, JAMB, IGCSE, and SAT curricula. Covers all major secondary school science topics with practical exam preparation.

📈

Performance Analytics

Track progress, scores, and time spent across all simulations. Teachers get actionable reports to identify strengths and weaknesses instantly.

🏆

Gamification System

Earn badges (10+ types), climb the leaderboard, level up with points, and maintain learning streaks. Motivates students to explore more.

👩‍🏫

Teacher Dashboard

Full classroom management: create classes, import students, assign simulations, build quizzes, auto-grade, and export reports — all free.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Portal

Parents monitor their child's progress — simulations completed, quiz scores, badges earned, and time spent learning.

🌐

Offline-First & Low-Bandwidth

~300KB total. Service Worker caching enables full offline use. Works on 2G and low-end devices — built for Nigerian realities.

🔬

Safe & Accessible

Perform dangerous experiments safely. Keyboard navigable. Screen reader friendly. No chemicals, no breakage, no risk.

🏢

Enterprise Features (Free)

School-wide deployment, admin dashboard, bulk CSV import, custom branding, white-label — all at zero cost. Built for Nigerian school systems.
