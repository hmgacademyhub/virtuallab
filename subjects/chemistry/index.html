Chemistry Lab Features

🧪 Safe Experiments

Perform dangerous or expensive experiments — like titration with concentrated acids — without physical risk. Perfect for pre-lab preparation.

📊 Data Collection

Record experimental data, generate titration curves, calculate concentrations, and export results for lab reports.

📚 Curriculum Aligned

Covers WAEC, NECO, JAMB, and IGCSE Chemistry (0620). Includes both theory and practical exam preparation.

Built-in quiz questions, observation checks, and calculation challenges with instant feedback.
