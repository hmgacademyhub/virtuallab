⚡ Ohm's Law & Electrical Circuits

Verify V = IR. Adjust voltage and resistance, observe current and power in an animated circuit.

📖 Theory: Ohm's Law states V = IR. The animated dots show electron flow. Current I = V/R, Power P = V×I = V²/R. This is a fundamental concept for WAEC/NECO physics.
