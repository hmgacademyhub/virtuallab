🔍 Lens Optics

Study image formation by a convex lens. Thin lens equation 1/f = 1/u + 1/v. WAEC/NECO practical.

📖 Theory: 1/f = 1/u + 1/v where f = focal length, u = object distance, v = image distance. When u > f, a real, inverted image is formed. When u = f, image is at infinity. When u < f, a virtual, upright image is formed.

🎯 WAEC Practical: A common WAEC/NECO physics practical involves determining the focal length of a converging lens using the lens formula.
