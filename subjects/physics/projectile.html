🎯 Projectile Motion

Study parabolic trajectories. Adjust launch angle and speed, observe range, height, and time of flight.

📖 Theory: A projectile follows a parabolic path under gravity. R = v₀²sin(2θ)/g, H = v₀²sin²θ/(2g).
🎯 JAMB Prep: Angle of 45° gives maximum range.
